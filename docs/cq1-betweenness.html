What this means

Standard campaign finance reporting counts dollars. This graph asks a different question: if a donor stopped giving, how much would the network fragment? Betweenness centrality answers that. The highest-scoring donors are the load-bearing walls of Johnston's political funding architecture — not necessarily its biggest spenders.
